Department of Computer Science

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in 2002 and aims to provide quality education in the field of computing. We focus on the holistic development of students by integrating academic excellence with practical skills.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to produce competent IT professionals who can contribute to society. The mission includes delivering strong theoretical foundations and hands-on experiences.
