Stay Healthy! Eat Well, Exercise Daily, Sleep Properly!

Health Awareness

Importance of Health:
Health is wealth. A healthy body and mind lead to a happy and successful life.

Tips for Staying Healthy:
- Eat a balanced diet with fruits and vegetables.
- Exercise at least 30 minutes daily.
- Drink plenty of water.
- Avoid junk food and sugary drinks.
- Sleep 7-8 hours every night.

Health Checkups:
Regular medical checkups help detect problems early and maintain good health.
